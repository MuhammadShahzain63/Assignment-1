Recipe of the day: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italion pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, panceta and pepper. It's simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion


Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 Large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino cheese
  • 50g Parmesan
  • Black pepper
  • Salt

Steps to prepare

  1. Boil the spaghetti is salted water until al dente
  2. Fry the pancetta until crispy
  3. Beat the eggs and mix grated cheese
  4. Drain the spaghetti and mix with pancetta
  5. Remove from heat and stir in the egg and cheese mixture
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediatly
